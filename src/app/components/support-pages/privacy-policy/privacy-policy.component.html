Who We Are

This portal is a government-supported initiative designed to empower local Indian vendors, particularly sellers of Geographical Indication (G-Tagged) products, to reach customers across the globe. We are committed to protecting the privacy of both our vendors and buyers while maintaining the highest standards of data security and transparency.

Empowering local vendors to showcase authentic Indian products globally

Information We Collect

We collect only the necessary information to provide you with the best possible service and to comply with legal requirements.

Personal Information

Name, address, phone number, email address

Order Details

Purchase history, product preferences, shopping behavior

Payment Information

UPI ID, bank details for refunds, masked card information

Location Data

Pin code, city, state for delivery optimization

Device & Log Information

IP address, device type, browser information, access times

We collect only necessary data to fulfill services efficiently and lawfully.

How We Use Your Information

Order Processing & Account Management

To process your orders, manage your account, and provide customer support

Vendor Verification & Authentication

To verify vendor credentials and ensure authentic G-tagged products

Delivery & Logistics

To facilitate efficient delivery and shipment tracking

Returns, Refunds & Customer Service

To handle returns, process refunds, and provide customer support

Service Updates & Important Alerts

To send essential service updates and important notifications (no spam)
